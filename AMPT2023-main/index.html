About The Event

The conference will encompass all the aspects of materials processing which starts from extraction to recycling. Participants of this conference will get an detailed exposure and deep insight of definition, solutions, developments and challenges of materials processing in current scenario. This conference will bring work being done in different streams of materials processing together.

Event venue location info and gallery

National Institute Of Technology Jamshedpur

National Institute of Technology, Jamshedpur earlier known as Regional Institute of Technology was established on 15th August 1960. It was among the first eight Regional Engineering Colleges (RECs) established as part of the Second Five-Year Plan (1956 - 1961). On 27th December 2002, RIT Jamshedpur was converted to National Institute of Technology Jamshedpur as per the decision of Govt. of India. Currently, NIT Jamshedpur is an institute of National Importance governed by ministry of education (MoE).
